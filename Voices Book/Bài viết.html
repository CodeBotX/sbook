My Favorite Movie: Titanic

Prompt: Write a paragraph (120 words) about your favorite movie, using the following prompts:

  • What is it?
  • What is the story of it?
  • When you watched it?
  • Why is it your favorite movie?

Detailed Outline

  1. What is it?
    • The name of the movie is Titanic.
    • It is a famous romantic movie.
  2. What is the story of it?
    • The movie is about Jack and Rose.
    • They meet on the ship called Titanic.
    • Jack is poor, Rose is rich, but they love each other.
    • The ship hits an iceberg and sinks. Jack dies, and Rose survives.
  3. When did you watch it?
    • I watched the movie last year.
    • I watched it with my family.
  4. Why is it your favorite movie?
    • I like it because the story is very good and touching.
    • The music in the movie is beautiful.
    • The actors act very well.
    • I never get bored when I watch it again.

Sample Paragraph

My favorite movie is Titanic. It is a love story between Jack and Rose. They meet on a big ship called the Titanic. Jack is a poor man, and Rose is a rich girl. They fall in love, but the ship hits an iceberg and sinks. Jack dies, and Rose lives. I watched this movie last year with my family. I liked the story very much. The movie is sad but very beautiful. The actors are very good. The music is also nice. I love the song "My Heart Will Go On." Titanic is my favorite movie because it has love, history, and strong feelings. I can watch it many times and never get bored.

Someone Who Inspires You: Thomas Edison

Prompt: Write a paragraph (100–120 words) about someone who inspires you. Use the prompts below:

  • Who is he/she?
  • What does he/she do?
  • Why do you admire him/her?
  • What can you learn from him/her?

Detailed Outline

  1. Who is he?
    • His name is Thomas Edison.
    • He was a famous inventor from the United States.
  2. What does he do?
    • He invented many things, such as the electric light bulb.
    • He worked very hard in his laboratory.
  3. Why do you admire him?
    • Because he was smart and never gave up.
    • He failed many times but still kept trying.
  4. What can you learn from him?
    • I learn to be patient and try hard every day.
    • Don’t be afraid of mistakes, always try to succeed.

Sample Paragraph

My inspiration is Thomas Edison. He was a famous inventor from the United States. He invented many things, but the most important is the electric light bulb. He worked very hard in his lab every day. I admire him because he was smart and never gave up. He failed many times, but he always tried again. I think that is very strong and brave. From Edison, I learn to be patient and keep trying, even when things are difficult. He shows me that we can do great things if we work hard. Thomas Edison is a great example for me, and I want to be like him in the future.

UNIT 8 – THE WORLD OF WORK: My Dream Job

Prompt: Write a paragraph (100–120 words) about your dream job. Use the prompts below:

  • What is your dream job?
  • What do you do in this job?
  • Why do you like this job?
  • Where would you like to work?

Detailed Outline

  1. What is your dream job?
    • My dream job is to be a software engineer or a game developer.
  2. What do you do in this job?
    • I would design, code, and test new features for games or software.
    • I would work on logical problems and find creative solutions.
  3. Why do you like this job?
    • It's a very creative field.
    • It involves problem-solving, which I enjoy.
    • There's always something new to learn.
    • I enjoy seeing ideas come to life through code.
  4. Where would you like to work?
    • In a modern, collaborative office environment.
    • Ideally, in a big tech hub with many innovative companies.

Sample Paragraph

My dream job is to be a software engineer. In this job, I would design and write computer programs, focusing on creating new applications or even video games. I imagine spending my days solving complex problems and turning creative ideas into functional code. I really like this job because it's intellectually stimulating and allows for endless creativity. There's always something new to learn, as technology is constantly evolving, which keeps me engaged. I would love to work in a modern, collaborative office, perhaps in a big city known for its tech industry. Being part of a team that builds innovative software that helps people or provides entertainment would be incredibly rewarding.

UNIT 9 – HEALTH AND HAPPINESS: How I Stay Happy and Healthy

Prompt: Write a paragraph (100–120 words) about how you stay happy and healthy. Use the prompts below:

  • What do you do to relax or feel good?
  • What helps you sleep well?
  • What do you eat or drink to stay healthy?
  • Why is it important to think positively?

Detailed Outline

  1. What do you do to relax or feel good?
    • Listen to music.
    • Take a walk in the park.
  2. What helps you sleep well?
    • Go to bed early.
    • Don’t use the phone before sleeping.
    • Drink warm milk at night.
  3. What do you eat or drink to stay healthy?
    • Eat fruits and vegetables.
    • Drink water every day.
    • Don’t eat fast food often.
  4. Why is it important to think positively?
    • It makes you feel happy.
    • You have more energy.
    • It helps you enjoy life.

Sample Paragraph

I want to stay happy and healthy. I relax by listening to music and walking in the park. It helps me feel good after a long day. I sleep well when I go to bed early and do not use my phone before sleeping. I also drink warm milk at night. To stay healthy, I eat a lot of fruits and vegetables. I drink water every day. I don’t eat too much fast food. I think positive thinking is very important. When I think positively, I feel happy and have more energy. It helps me enjoy life more. I feel better when I take care of myself like this.

UNIT 10 – ENTERTAINMENT: My Favorite Way to Have Fun

Prompt: Write a paragraph (100–120 words) about your favorite way to have fun. Use the prompts below:

  • What do you do in your free time?
  • How often do you do it?
  • Why do you enjoy it?
  • Who do you usually do it with?

Detailed Outline

  1. What do you do in your free time?
    • Playing online video games.
  2. How often do you do it?
    • Almost every evening for about an hour.
    • More often on weekends.
  3. Why do you enjoy it?
    • It's a great way to relax and unwind after a busy day.
    • It offers mental challenges and strategic thinking.
    • It's exciting to achieve goals within the game.
  4. Who do you usually do it with?
    • Mostly with online friends, often those who live far away.
    • Sometimes with my younger brother.

Sample Paragraph

In my free time, my absolute favorite way to have fun is playing online video games. I usually engage in this hobby almost every evening for around an hour, and I spend more time on it during weekends. I enjoy it immensely because it serves as a fantastic way to relax and de-stress after a busy day. The games often present interesting puzzles and strategic challenges, which keep my mind active and engaged. It's also a wonderful platform to connect with my friends who might live far away; we can chat and laugh together while playing, making it feel like we're in the same room. Occasionally, my younger brother also joins our gaming sessions, adding to the fun and excitement.

UNIT 11 – CHALLENGES: A Challenge in My Life

Prompt: Write a paragraph (100–120 words) about a challenge in your life. Use the prompts below:

  • What was the challenge?
  • Was it a physical or mental challenge?
  • How did you try to overcome it?
  • What did you learn from it?

Detailed Outline

  1. What was the challenge?
    • Learning to speak English.
    • It was difficult to speak in front of others.
  2. Was it a physical or mental challenge?
    • It was a mental challenge.
  3. How did you try to overcome it?
    • I practiced speaking every day.
    • I watched English videos and repeated the words.
    • I joined a speaking club at school.
  4. What did you learn from it?
    • I learned to be more confident.
    • I learned that practice helps a lot.

Sample Paragraph

One challenge in my life was learning to speak English. I was very shy and afraid to talk in front of people. It was a mental challenge for me. I wanted to improve, so I practiced speaking English every day. I watched short English videos and repeated the words. I also joined a speaking club at my school. After some time, I felt more confident and happy. I can now speak better than before. I learned that practice is very important and that it’s okay to make mistakes. This challenge helped me grow and become stronger.

UNIT 12 – TECHNOLOGY: Technology in My Daily Life

Prompt: Write a paragraph (100–120 words) about technology in your daily life. Use the prompts below:

  • How often do you use the internet or apps?
  • What do you use them for?
  • What is your favorite app or device?
  • Why can’t you live without it?

Detailed Outline

  1. How often do you use the internet or apps?
    • I use the internet every day.
    • I check apps many times a day.
  2. What do you use them for?
    • For learning, watching videos, chatting with friends, and playing games.
  3. What is your favorite app or device?
    • My favorite app is YouTube.
    • I also like using my smartphone.
  4. Why can’t you live without it?
    • It helps me learn new things.
    • It keeps me connected with people.
    • It makes my life easier and more fun.

Sample Paragraph

Technology is very important in my daily life. I use the internet and apps every day. I use them to learn English, watch videos, talk to my friends, and play games. My favorite app is YouTube because I can watch many fun and useful videos. I also love my smartphone. I can do almost everything on it. I can study, listen to music, take photos, and send messages. I can’t live without my phone because it helps me stay connected and learn new things. Technology makes my life easier, faster, and more interesting. I think it is a big part of my life now.
