The director’s role is to direct the film to ensure it meets expectations. Many films premiere at festivals before being released to a wider audience, allowing people to experience them enjoyably.

Người B: Chưa, nhưng tôi sẽ mua ngay. Tôi hy vọng chúng ta sẽ enjoyably watch nó. Ai direct bộ phim này vậy?

Bài đọc liên quan

Regular visits to the dentist's office are important for oral health. During your appointment, the dentist will examine your teeth for any signs of a cavity. A routine cleaning helps remove plaque and tartar. If you have a cavity, the dentist will fill it to prevent further decay. In some cases, a tooth might need an extraction, which means the dentist will extract it. For missing teeth, dental implant procedures are available. Remember to clean your teeth daily.

Người B: Đúng vậy. Và nếu bạn mất răng, họ cũng có thể implant răng mới. Quan trọng là phải clean răng thường xuyên.

Bài đọc liên quan

A pharmacy plays an essential role in healthcare. When you have a doctor's prescription, the pharmacist will dispense the correct medication. They ensure the dosage is accurate and provide instructions on how to administer it safely. You can also ask them to refill your prescription when needed. For minor ailments, many over-the-counter medications are available without a prescription. It's important to discontinue use and consult a doctor if you experience any adverse reactions.
